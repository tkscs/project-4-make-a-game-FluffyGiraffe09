Ambitious goal:
    I want a game where there is a candy jar at the bottom of the screen that you can move from left to right. Then various things fall from the sky (some good and some bad) that you can catch in the jar. Some of the objects falling will be candies, and others will be "bad" objects that aren't supposed to go in. The different objects will fall faster as you keep going, and every time you catch a good item you get a point. If you accidentally catch a bad item you lose. Coins also fall every so often. there is a seperate tally for the amount of coins you catch. Once you reach a certain amount of coins, power ups start falling from the sky also (for example a ton of candy starts falling).

    - theme: Candy
    - Player interactions: arrow keys, side to side
    - winning: You don't win the game once you reach a certain score, you just keep playing until you catch a bad object.
